# CONTRACT.md — briefs/

## Purpose
Short synthesized state-of-X notes.

## Agent behavior
- Write for fast human reading.
- Include what changed, current status, risks, and next clean move.
- Cite receipts where available.

## Authority level
Summary; receipts and accepted decisions outrank briefs.

## Movement rules
Promote durable, stable knowledge to data/wiki when it stops being temporary.

## Hard stops
Do not store secrets or raw private dumps here.
