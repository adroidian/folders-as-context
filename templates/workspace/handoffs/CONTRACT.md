# CONTRACT.md — handoffs/

## Purpose
Temporary next-session context when wiki + receipts are insufficient.

## Agent behavior
- Keep handoffs short, actionable, and dated.
- Include status, decisions needed, blockers, files touched, and verification.

## Authority level
Temporary context.

## Movement rules
Distill durable facts to data/wiki and archive stale handoffs.

## Hard stops
Do not use handoffs as permanent memory dumps.
